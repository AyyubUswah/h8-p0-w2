Dik :
  resultant force = mass x acceleration 
  mass = 600 kg
  acceleration = 2 m/s2
Dit :
  Find the resultant force ?

<--pseudo code-->

START  

STORE "mass" with 600
STORE "accelation" with 2

CALCULATE "mass" times "acceleration"
STORE "resultant force" to the result of calculation

DISPLAY "resultant force" in " kg.m/s2"
END

START

STORE "years" with any value

CALCULATE "years" mod 4
CALCULATE "years" mod 100

STORE "remainder" to the result of calculation

IF "remainder" of mod 100 not equal 0 but "remainder" of mod 4 equal 0
  DISPLAY "kabisat year"
